The Origin

Born from a late-night conversation and a shared obsession with the perfect ratios,
Velvet Lounge was established to honor the timeless ritual of the cocktail.
We don't just serve drinks, we curate experiences that linger long after the glass is empty.
From the first drop to the final sip, every element is a tribute to the golden age of spirits.

The Art of the Pour

The Alchemy:

  • The Ice:

    We believe dilution is an art. Our crystal-clear, hand-carved blocks are frozen at specific temperatures to ensure your drink stays chilled without losing its soul.

  • The Bitters:

    House-pressed and aged in small batches, our tinctures provide the botanical backbone to our signature menu.

  • The Spirits:

    A library of rare finds and local legends. Every bottle on our backbar is selected for its character, heritage, and story.
